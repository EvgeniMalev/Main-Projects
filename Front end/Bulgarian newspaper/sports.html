Regional rugby league: a brief history

The inaugural competition for amateur rugby teams in Veliko Tarnovo Province kicked off in the late 1950s. Yantra Draganovo emerged victorious in the first tournament, clinching the title. Subsequent editions saw teams like Botev Vishovgrad, Levski Polski Senoets, and CSKA Samovodene competing fiercely for glory. In 1958, Yantra Draganovo secured the championship with an impressive display of skill and teamwork, setting the tone for years to come. The matches were held at various stadiums across Veliko Tarnovo, with enthusiastic crowds cheering on their favorite teams. As the league gained popularity, the level of competition intensified, leading to memorable clashes and thrilling encounters.

Statistics:

  • Year: 1958
  • Champion: Yantra Draganovo
  • Runner-up: Botev Vishovgrad
  • Top Scorer: Ivan Petrov (Levski Polski Senoets) - 12 tries
  • Best Defense: CSKA Samovodene - conceded only 3 tries
