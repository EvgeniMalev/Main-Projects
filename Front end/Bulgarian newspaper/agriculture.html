Birds heaven

A new ecopark for domestic birds is being created. Over 250 types of domestic birds will be bred and live freely in it, such as: chickens, ducks, turkeys, guinea fowl, pigeons and others. The park will spread over 3,000 acres of land, and will have its own ecosystem consisting of a lake, a flowing river, coniferous and deciduous forests.

Bulgarian Farmers Embrace Sustainable Agriculture Practices Amid Climate Concerns

As Bulgaria grapples with the impact of climate change on its agricultural sector, farmers across the country are increasingly turning towards sustainable farming practices. With extreme weather events becoming more frequent and unpredictable, there is a growing recognition among farmers of the need to adapt. From embracing organic farming methods to implementing water-saving techniques and crop diversification, Bulgarian agriculture is undergoing a significant transformation. Government initiatives and EU subsidies are also incentivizing farmers to adopt sustainable practices, promoting soil health and biodiversity conservation. This shift not only benefits the environment but also enhances the resilience of Bulgaria's agricultural sector in the face of climate challenges, ensuring food security and sustainable livelihoods for future generations.

Digital Innovation Revolutionizes Bulgarian AgriTech Sector

In a bid to boost efficiency and productivity, Bulgaria's agriculture industry is witnessing a surge in digital innovation and technology adoption. From precision farming and automated machinery to smart irrigation systems and data analytics, farmers are embracing cutting-edge solutions to optimize their operations. This digital revolution is not only streamlining processes and reducing costs but also enabling better decision-making through data-driven insights. Start-ups and tech firms are playing a pivotal role in this transformation, developing innovative solutions tailored to the specific needs of Bulgarian agriculture. Moreover, government support and investment incentives are further fueling the growth of the AgriTech sector, positioning Bulgaria as a hub for agricultural innovation in the region. As the industry continues to evolve, these technological advancements promise to drive sustainable growth and competitiveness in Bulgarian agriculture.
